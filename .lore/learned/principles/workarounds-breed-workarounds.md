The `ARTIFACT_DOCUMENT_WRITE_OP` array-join dodge survived three commissions before being traced to a substring-matching security hook in user-owned plugin space. The codebase had been training contributors to obfuscate strings whenever tooling misfired. Explicit "Fix the Problem, Not the Symptom" rule citation surfaces these — without it, each contributor reapplies the workaround.
