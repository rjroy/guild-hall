---
title: When a refactor feels too big, look for a smaller extraction first
date: 2026-04-28
status: active
tags: [refactor, planning]
modules: []
---

Prerequisite refactors compound. A large refactor often becomes feasible only because earlier extractions factored out the hard dependencies. When a refactor feels too big, check whether a smaller extraction would make it tractable before attempting the whole thing.
