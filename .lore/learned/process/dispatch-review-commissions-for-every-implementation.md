---
title: Dispatch review commissions for every implementation, including "simple" ones
date: 2026-04-28
status: active
tags: [process, commissions, review]
modules: []
---

Skipping review on UI-only or "simple" work breaks the spec-to-plan-to-implement-to-review chain. Consistency matters more than per-commission risk assessment; fresh-context verification catches gaps the implementer missed regardless of perceived complexity.
