---
title: Action plans should state what will happen, not what won't
date: 2026-04-28
status: active
tags: [planning, process]
modules: []
---

Spec-phase hedging belongs in the spec; once planning commits, alternatives are noise. Cut from plans: "Spec Gaps Surfaced During Planning" and "Out of Scope" sections, inline conditional phrases ("if X prefers different..."), and references tracking deferred decisions. Operational detail like the Rollback Plan stays.
